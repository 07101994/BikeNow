Pronto Cycle with style on

Bike Now is the best way to enjoy Seattle's bike sharing system.
Packed with tons of features and a modern Android interface, you will feel right at home.

Features

Pronto at a Glance

Visualize the entire Pronto Cycle network in real time.

Station pins will display the amount of bikes remaining using quick color feedback.

Rich Station Information

Individual station information is concisely presented with live availability of bikes and racks.

Google Street View preview will help you pinpoint the location of each station with its surrounding environment. You can also open the station right into the Google Maps application (or other compatible apps) for further processing.

See What's Around

Ever been to your favorite station to drop your bike just to find it full?

Worry no more! Stations closest to you are automatically displayed in the app menu so that you can quickly find an alternate location.

You've Got Favorites

You can add stations to your favorites list right from the station information. They can then be accessed from the menu into a summary view containing all the relevant data for each of them.

Inspired by the wonderful Moyeu

Bike Now was inspired by -and could not not have been made without- the help of Jérémie Laval and his wonderful companion for Boston's Hubway, Moyeu.

If you are in the Boston area be sure to download Moyeu for free on Google Play.

Free and Open-Source Software

The entire Bike Now source code is available on GitHub under the open-source Apache 2 license. Since Bike Now uses the Xamarin platform, it's a good playground to build your own apps.
